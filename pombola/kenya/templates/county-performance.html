How are our taxpayer shillings really being spent?

A recent report by Kenya’s Controller of Budget highlights questionable spending practices in our counties. Over 50% of our counties spent NO money on development!

The report reveals that our taxpayer money that this is supposed to be spent on building roads and water systems as well as equipping and staffing health centres and schools, has instead been spent on “construction of mansions, entertainment allowances and buying of luxurious vehicles for Governors and their executives” (source). Only 7% of county government budgets was spent on development programmes!
